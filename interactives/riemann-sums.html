Key Formulas

Width of Subinterval
$$ \Delta x = \frac{b - a}{n} $$

Definite Integral
$$ \int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = \lim_{n \to \infty} \sum_{i=1}^{n} f(x_i^*) \Delta x $$

Midpoint Approximation
$$ M_n = \sum_{i=1}^{n} f(\bar{x}_i) \Delta x $$
