Titration Guide

Meniscus Reading: Volumetric glass is designed to be read from the bottom curve of the liquid level (the meniscus).

Perfect Endpoint: Phenolphthalein turns a delicate faint pink when the solution is very slightly basic (pH 8.2 – 10.0). Adding too much base saturates the indicator, turning the solution dark pink or magenta.

Volumetric Formula:

Strength of Acid × Volume of Acid = Strength of Base × Volume of Base
